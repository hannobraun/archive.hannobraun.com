Weekly Release - Quick and Painless

Posted on 2023-01-02 by Hanno Braun

Fornjot is an early-stage project to create a next-generation Code-CAD application. This is the announcement for this week's release, version 0.30.0.

This one's going to be quick and painless: Last week I was on vacation and I lost part of the week before due to getting sick. As a result, not a whole lot happened. I'll have more to say next week!
